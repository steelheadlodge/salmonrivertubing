Good to Know

How long is the float?

Typically 2–3 hours depending on current flow. Perfect half-day adventure.

Is it safe for kids?

Yes! At 250–600 CFS it's gentle and safe for all ages. Check our live tubing status before booking.

What should I wear?

Swimsuit, water shoes or sandals, sunscreen. Bring a dry bag for valuables. We provide life jackets.

Can I bring my own tubes?

Yes — our shuttle service is available even if you bring your own gear. Call to arrange.

Do you take reservations?

Walk-ins welcome, but calling ahead is recommended for groups of 4 or more. (315) 298-4371

About the Lodge

Right on the River.
The Best Float in Pulaski.

Situated directly on the world-renowned Salmon River in Pulaski, NY, Steelhead Lodge is your summer home base for river tubing, kayaking, and home-cooked meals. Dinner is served every evening — and when Joe's Smokehouse is open, BBQ ribs and pulled pork are on the menu too.

We're not a resort — we're a river lodge. Your tubes launch right from the bank. And the river sounds make for a pretty great soundtrack all night long.
