Why Float With Us

The Best Float on the Salmon River

We're not a generic outfitter — we're right on the water at Steelhead Lodge, with local knowledge, easy access, and everything you need for a perfect day on the river.

The Salmon River in Pulaski, NY is world-famous for its trophy fishing — but from late spring through summer, it's one of the most beautiful float rivers in the Northeast. Crystal clear runs, wooded banks, and gentle currents make it perfect for tubing and kayaking with the whole family.

Operating directly from Steelhead Lodge, we offer the most convenient access on the river. No driving to distant launch points — just gear up, hop in, and float. Our free shuttle service picks you up downstream so you can relax from start to finish.
